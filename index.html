One solution!

One solution will let you Uncover the real value of your vehicles!

With an efficient vehicle tracking system you don’t only reduce the operational costs, but you increase customer satisfaction, enhance business performance, and eventually increase revenue. The data about your vehicle efficiency and driver productivity will let you:

  • Control car Idle time
  • Cut unnecessary drivers overtime
  • Add more jobs to each work day
  • Reduce fuel consumption
  • Reduce maintenance costs
  • Route optimization, better delivery time.
  • Reduced management cost
  • Real time intervention in emergencies (reduce risks)
  • Restore stolen cars

Get more revenue from the same vehicles you have, now!

Track your vehicles

Manage your drivers, enhance security and safety!

Whether you want to know the accurate delivery time, the momentarily status of the vehicle, or you want to keep an eye on the cargo, Express will get you covered. You will know exactly who’s driving your car, his overall performance, and you’ll get notified about any unplanned stop. Also, you can control your vehicles remotely if you suspect something, you can immobilize your vehicle, or get access to the OBD (Vehicles internal system).

  • Get management Reports
  • Route planning and optimization
  • Special sensors monitoring
  • Driver management
  • Communication with drivers
  • Automatic driver identification system (I button)
  • Driving behavior analysis
  • Driving times monitoring
  • Security
  • Unexpected events alerts
  • Fuel management
  • Remote vehicle immobilization
  • Access vehicles control system

Express tracking systems will let you control all your vehicles from your desktop.

Do more for the community!

As an expression of gratitude to the community in which we live, we are committed to help entities in reducing the greenhouse emissions and fuel consumption of their vehicles, and poor driving behavior such as speeding, unnecessary idling, and car drifting wastes fuel and can be controlled by an Express GPS tracking system. Every unnecessary KM you eliminate will help in making your entire fleet more productive and environmentally responsible and will help the community in achieving the sustainability vision.

  •  Efficient fuel consumption (vehicle performance)
  • Optimized routes (eliminate unnecessary KMs)
  • Expand geographically
  • Reduce CO2 emissions
